Welcome to Generala Game

Game Rules:

  • It can be played by two or more players.

  • A player may choose which dices to enroll or to keep.

  • A player may reroll some or all of the dices up to three times on a turn.

  • If a player makes straight, Full house or Four of a kind on the first roll of a given turn, it is worth 5 or 10 extra points.

  • A player who makes Generala on the first roll of a turn qutomatically wins th game.

  • A category is eliminated if a player fails to make a valid score or chooses not to take score. And he can not score on that category for the rest of the game.

  • Winner of the game is the one who finishes with the most points unless one scores General of their first roll.
